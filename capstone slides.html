Why Predict?

Isn't is awesome when someone can read your mind? Okay, maybe we can't do that right now BUT what we can do is predict what you will type next...

so when you type: i love...

our first prediction would be YOU

BUT How Does It Work?

...hit next arrow...

You enter a word or a phrase

Our application runs the magic algorithm (pre-modeled by huge datasets from news, blogs and twitter taken from HC Corpora)

Our application returns up-to five possible predictions

It even lets you select the word and continue searching until your sentence is complete

Algorithm?

The application is pre-modeled and trained by N-grams made from huge datasets

The datasets had to be cleaned before they could be trained

We then built a function search, for prediction, using Basic NLP and utilizing N-grams in Markov Model and Back-off Model

The TM Package was used extensively in creating the Application

Why Dont You Try It Yourself?

The application is deployed at ShinyApps.io*

Wait for the application to load

Begin typing word(s) in the provided text box

The word(s) will be searched for the next best prediction

The predictions will be shown with your typed phrase. You can select one of the predictions to copy it in the text field and the next prediction will be automaticaly searched
